Example Scams (what to look for)

Fake bank email

Asks to "verify" your password — real banks never ask for passwords via email.

Spoofed login page

URL doesn't match the site name — always check the address bar before signing in.

Urgent SMS

Threats or immediate actions — slow down and verify via official channels.
